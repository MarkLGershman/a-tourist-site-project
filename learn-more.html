What is in an Ottawa Hometown Travel Itinerary?

  • Interesting day trips and excursions.
  • Recommendations for which tourist sites are better than other ones.
  • Inside information on what is going on in entertainment and sports viewing.
  • Recommendations for local daytime activities, restaurants, and nightlife.
  • Locations, contact information, pricing, previews, and timing tips for each itinerary item.

How Do I Buy An Itinerary That Is Best For Me?

Step 1: Choose your customer type

Family

Your non stop discovery of what is fun and interesting about Ottawa for your family! Avoid long walks and boring sightseeing. Learn what families in ottawa do for fun.

Business

Take advantage of your free time on your busy trip. No more sitting in the hotel room and ordering room service. Learn where ottawa business people go for good food, and lively entertainment.

Adventure

Your passion for outdoor adventure, hiking, kayaking, skating, skeeing, and biking are just outside your hotel door in ottawa. Plan a quick bike excursion or your next canoe trip with us.

Step 2: Choose which Itinerary type is best for you?

Standard Itineraries

One and two day itineraries are pre-defined and available at a set price. There will always be one featured itinerary per customer type described above, but these are offered and available to everyone. They are renewed on a regular basis with new recommendations and the latest updates.

Customized Itineraries

Available for an additional charge. Each customized itinerary is designed to match location, cost, time and activity preferences. A questionnaire is provided to capture this information and guide the design of the itinerary. Multiple day visits and extended excursions can be planned with Customized Itineraries.
